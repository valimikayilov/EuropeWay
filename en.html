MODUL University

Vienna, Austria

Modul University is one of the oldest private universities in Austria. It was found in 1908 and still keeps reputation of top 25 best performing universities in Austria. Modul University also provides its own accommodation with affordable prices and good location close to the city center. Another huge advantage of studying in Modul University it is big amount of partnership companies all over the world, which are able to offer internship and possibility of further employment.
